Complex Analytic Landscapes


Complex functions $ f:\mathbb{C}\to\mathbb{C} $ can be visualized by plotting the function $$\tilde f: \mathbb{R^2} \to \mathbb{R}\quad \text{with}\quad \tilde f(x,y) = |f\left(x + i y\right)|\quad \text{in 3D}.$$ The color of a patch $\left(x, y, |f(x + i y)|\right)$ indicates the phase of the complex number $f(x + i y)$.
